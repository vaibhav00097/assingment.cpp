Overview of Hotel Maharani

Hotel Maharani Nainital - commissioned in 1991, which is one of the finest resort of the Kumaon Region and closest hill stations to Delhi & Lucknow (six hours Jouney by road). The Hotel was built keeping 5 star standard in mind so that services of highest standards be provided to our valued customers. We are also proud to tell you that we are the only hotel at Nainital having a licensed Bar (The Bar) and offering 5-star facilities. The hotel building overlooks the Nainital Valley and offers a panoramic view of the magnificent Naini Lake from each of its room – The Hotel Maharani at present stands in place of cottage of legendary British hunter and environmentalist Jim Corbett.

The hotel was out shopping for its new look for almost a year & now it is back with a spanking appearance. With a blend of contemporary & vintage style, the interiors of the recently refurbished hotel command a great reception. It opens with a gentle lobby that casts vibes of a warm welcome, typical of Hotel Maharani hospitality. The hotel offers a wide variety of luxury accommodation to choose from. At Hotel Maharani Nainital all 65 rooms & the luxury suites pledge to give you an alluring view of the sparkling Lake Naini surrounded by the handsome Himalayas. The rooms also promise to give you a bouquet of fresh services such as mini bar, direct dial telephone with international access, plasma TV & a twenty-four hour in room food & beverage service.
